ABOUT OUR SERVER

Mythic City is an immersive Minecraft roleplay and survival server that invites players to experience a vibrant, bustling urban
environment filled with endless possibilities.Set in a meticulously crafted cityscape, players can build their own stories while
navigating through diverse neighborhoods, each with unique architecture and themes. Engage in thrilling roleplay scenarios,
from bustling marketplaces to hidden alleyways, all while collaborating with fellow players to create a thriving community.
With survival elements integrated into the gameplay, you’ll need to gather resources, fend off challenges, and establish
your place in this dynamic metropolis.Whether you're a seasoned roleplayer or new to the scene, Mythic City offers a captivating
blend of creativity, adventure, and camaraderie that makes every visit a new journey.

RULES

RESPECT ALL PLAYERS

No bullying, harassment, or discrimination.

Keep chat friendly and avoid offensive language.

No spamming in chat or private messages.

NO GRIFING OR RAIDING

Don’t destroy or steal from others’ builds or property unless the server is designed for PvP or raiding.

Respect other players’ space and work.

NO CHEATING AND EXPLOITING

No use of hacked clients, X-ray, or unauthorized mods.

Don’t exploit bugs or glitches to gain an unfair advantage.

Use only approved mods (list them if there are any).

KEEP THE ENVIRONMENT CLEAN

No excessive land claiming or unnecessary destruction.

Avoid leaving random floating trees, abandoned structures, or holes in the ground.

Replant trees or crops if farming.

PvP RULES

PvP (Player vs. Player) should only happen in designated areas, or if both players agree.

No spawn-killing or ambushing players repeatedly.

RESPECT SERVER BUILDINGS AND COMMUNITY PROJECTS

Avoid modifying community builds without permission.

Help maintain community areas by following posted guidelines or rules.

NO ADs

Don’t promote other servers or external websites in chat or messages.

Respect the community and avoid self-promotion unless permitted.

FOLLOW BUILDING GUIDELINE

Build at a respectful distance from others’ builds.

Avoid towering structures or redstone machines that can cause lag unless approved.

NO EXCESSIVE LAG-CAUSING ACTIVITES

Avoid large numbers of entities (animals, mobs) or automated farms that can slow down the server.

Keep redstone clocks or machines within reasonable limits.

LISTEN TO MODs & ADMINs

Follow the instructions of staff members and respect their decisions.

Report any issues, but don’t argue with or disrespect the staff.

KEEP THE SERVER FAMILY-FRIENDLY (mandtory)

Avoid inappropriate topics, skins, or usernames.

Keep the language and behavior clean for all ages.{mandtory}

USE COMMOM SENSE

Play fair, be kind, and make the experience enjoyable for everyone.

If unsure about something, ask an admin or refer to the server’s specific guidelines.
